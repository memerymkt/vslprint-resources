Professional Laminating for Every Need

When documents need to last, New York businesses turn to professional laminating services. Whether you're protecting restaurant menus from daily handling, preserving important certificates, or creating durable instructional signage for your facility, quality lamination makes the difference between a document that lasts weeks and one that lasts years.

VSL Print offers thermal laminating in multiple thicknesses — from lightweight 3-mil for basic protection to heavy-duty 10-mil for items that need to withstand constant handling. Our equipment handles documents from business card size up to 60-inch wide format posters, all with crystal-clear finish or anti-glare matte options.

Laminating Options and Applications

Pouch Laminating

Ideal for individual items like ID badges, reference cards, luggage tags, and small signs. Available in gloss or matte finish with rounded corner cutting included. Same-day service available for urgent needs.

Roll Laminating

For larger items and high-volume runs — posters, maps, charts, architectural drawings, and retail displays. Our wide-format roll laminators handle materials up to 60 inches wide with precision heat and pressure control.

UV Coating

Ultra-violet coating provides a high-gloss, scuff-resistant finish without the thickness of traditional lamination. Popular for business cards, brochures, and marketing collateral where a premium feel matters.

Encapsulation

Full-edge sealing that extends beyond the document border, providing waterproof protection on all sides. Essential for outdoor signage, construction site documents, and marine/industrial applications.

Who Uses Laminating Services in NYC?

Our laminating services support a wide range of industries across the five boroughs:

  • Restaurants and bars — Menu lamination is our highest-volume service. NYC's 27,000+ restaurants need menus that survive spills, handling, and frequent seasonal updates.
  • Schools and universities — Classroom materials, hall passes, reference charts, and library resources all benefit from durable lamination.
  • Real estate firms — Property spec sheets, floor plans, and neighborhood maps displayed at open houses need to look pristine through dozens of showings.
  • Healthcare facilities — Protocol cards, patient information sheets, and wayfinding signage require wipe-clean surfaces that meet hygiene standards.
  • Construction and architecture — Blueprint protection, site safety signage, and project documentation that needs to survive job site conditions.
