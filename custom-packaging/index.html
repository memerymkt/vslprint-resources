Packaging That Tells Your Brand Story

In a city where first impressions drive commerce, your packaging is often the first physical touchpoint between your brand and your customer. From the moment a box arrives at someone's door to the instant a shopper lifts a product off a shelf, packaging creates an experience that either builds loyalty or gets forgotten.

VSL Print designs and manufactures custom packaging for businesses across New York City — from e-commerce mailer boxes that survive the journey from warehouse to doorstep, to luxury retail packaging that commands premium pricing on the shelf. We handle structural design, graphic production, die cutting, and finishing all within our Manhattan facility.

Packaging Types We Produce

Retail & Product Boxes

Custom folding cartons, rigid setup boxes, and display packaging for consumer products. Full CMYK printing with specialty finishes — soft touch, spot UV, metallic foil. From cosmetics and supplements to electronics and artisanal food products.

E-Commerce Mailer Boxes

Custom-printed corrugated mailers designed for shipping durability and unboxing experience. Interior printing, tissue paper wraps, and branded tape options. Sized to minimize dimensional weight shipping charges.

Food & Beverage Packaging

FDA-compliant packaging for restaurants, bakeries, coffee roasters, and food producers. Grease-resistant coatings, window die cuts, and food-safe inks. Supporting NYC's legendary food scene one box at a time.

Luxury & Gift Boxes

Rigid boxes with magnetic closures, ribbon pulls, and custom inserts. Wrapped in specialty papers with debossing, letterpress, or hand-applied details. For jewelry, spirits, high-end retail, and corporate gifting.

NYC Industries We Package For

  • Fashion and apparel — Garment boxes, shoe boxes, and branded tissue for the city's fashion brands from emerging designers to established houses.
  • Cosmetics and beauty — Compact packaging, subscription box components, and counter displays that meet the beauty industry's exacting visual standards.
  • Food and hospitality — Takeout containers, bakery boxes, coffee sleeves, and catering packaging for NYC's 27,000+ restaurants and food establishments.
  • Cannabis — Compliant child-resistant packaging with premium branding for New York's regulated cannabis market.
  • Tech and electronics — Protective packaging with custom foam inserts, magnetic closures, and clean modern graphics for consumer electronics brands.

Prototyping and Short Runs

Launching a new product? Testing a rebrand? We produce packaging prototypes and short runs starting at just 50 units. Our digital die-cutting and printing equipment lets you test designs, materials, and structures before committing to a full production order.

For established products, we run quantities from 500 to 50,000+ with offset printing for the sharpest color reproduction and most competitive per-unit pricing at volume.
