Direct Mail That Actually Gets Opened

In the age of overflowing email inboxes, physical mail has become the unexpected advantage. Direct mail response rates consistently outperform digital channels — the Data & Marketing Association reports a 9% response rate for house lists versus 1% for email. In a market as competitive as New York City, that difference translates directly to revenue.

VSL Print handles every step of direct mail campaigns: design, printing, list management, addressing, sorting, postage optimization, and USPS delivery. We're a one-stop facility that eliminates the coordination headaches of juggling separate designers, printers, and mail houses.

Direct Mail Services

Every Door Direct Mail (EDDM)

USPS EDDM lets you saturate specific postal routes without purchasing a mailing list. Choose your target neighborhoods by zip code, demographics, or proximity to your business. Starting at just $0.20 per piece for postage — the most cost-effective way to reach every household and business on a mail route.

Targeted List Mailing

Purchase or use your own mailing lists for precision targeting. We handle CASS certification, NCOA processing, deduplication, and presort optimization to maximize deliverability and minimize postage costs. Variable data printing lets you personalize each piece.

Postcards & Self-Mailers

High-impact postcards in sizes from 4x6 to jumbo 6x11. Full-color front and back on heavy card stock with UV coating for durability. Self-mailers with tear-off reply cards, perforated coupons, or fold-out panels for maximum engagement.

Letters & Envelopes

Personalized letters with matching envelopes, inserts, and reply devices. Variable data printing for individualized messaging. Available with first-class or standard postage with full tracking and delivery confirmation.

NYC Businesses That Win With Direct Mail

  • Restaurants and local services — EDDM campaigns targeting postal routes within a 1-3 mile radius of your location. New restaurant openings, seasonal menus, and local service promotions.
  • Real estate agents — Just-listed, just-sold, and market update mailers to farm specific neighborhoods. Personalized with MLS data and agent branding.
  • Medical and dental practices — New patient acquisition mailers to surrounding residential areas. HIPAA-compliant for appointment reminders and health communications.
  • Political campaigns — Voter contact mailers with variable data for targeted messaging by district, party, or voting history. We handle NYC Board of Elections compliance requirements.
  • Nonprofits — Fundraising appeals, event invitations, and annual reports mailed to donor lists with personalized ask amounts and giving history references.

Postage Optimization Saves You Money

Postage is typically the largest cost in a direct mail campaign — often more than printing. Our presorting and commingling processes can reduce your postage by 30-50% compared to first-class rates. We maintain USPS Business Mail Entry Unit (BMEU) access for same-day mail entry and faster delivery.

For large campaigns, we provide detailed postage reports showing exact costs by mail class, presort tier, and delivery zone. No surprises on your invoice.
